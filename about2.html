About Us

Goal Vision Mission Quality Policy

Indiacom Limited (formerly known as Sesa Seat Information Systems Limited) was formed in technical & financial collaboration between Sesa Goa Limited and STET S.p.a. of Italy (a Division of IRI) on 21st of January 1988 to add wider dimension to the Information Industry in India.

In the Information Business, Indiacom’s main objective is to acquire, validate & compile authentic business information for dissemination through Online Search portal www.Indiacom.com, Printed Business directories and Mobile applications adapting LBS Technology. Indiacom also Licenses its IPR protected Content for various applications in the Online and LBS domain.

Pan India Presence: Indiacom – A professionally managed company, having a network of branches across India with a dominant presence in Western India.

Indiacom believes in providing complete and comprehensive information in the most easy-to-refer formats and this has been the endeavor all along. This is manifested through its product line.
