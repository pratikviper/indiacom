History of Indiacom

Milestones that shaped who we are.

  1. 1988 — Incorporation

    Incorporated as Sesa Seat INFORMATION Systems
    JV: Sesa Goa & Seat S.P.A. Italy (60:40).
    Core activities: Telecom data compilation & directory publishing, business data collection and Yellow Pages with advertising.

  2. 1988–1997 — Transition & MBO

    Parent companies withdrew (Italian Govt. decision). Completed India’s first Management Buyout.
    Name changed to Indiacom & geographical expansion.

  3. 1998 — Brand & Format

    Enlarged business database (B2B & B2C). Continued TD & YP publishing in a new format. Strengthened the brand identity ‘Indiacom’.

  4. 1999–2000 — Online Foray

    Dominated TD+YP market in West India. Launched web portal Indiacom.com.
    Digital offerings to SMBs (e.g., “Meri Website”). Hyperlocal initiatives (Nearbuy).

  5. 2000–2008 — Digital Enablement

    Content licensing and digital enablement services for SMBs.

  6. 2008 — Data Products & Analytics

    Hyperlocal data products (APIs & intelligent insights); big-data analytics and prescriptions.

  7. 2023 — Present

    Continuing the journey of digital products, insights, and value creation.
