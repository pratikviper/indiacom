Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming India's trusted digital partner for thousands of businesses

1988 - The Beginning

Founded in 1988, Indiacom Private Limited pioneered the concept of Telephone Directories and Yellow Pages in India, establishing the foundation for local business discovery.

2001 - Digital Transformation

With the shift to digital, we launched our Internet Yellow Pages in 2001, helping thousands of SMBs take their first steps online and embrace the digital revolution.

2011 - Google Partnership

In 2011, Indiacom became one of the earliest partners to introduce Google 360¬∞ Business Photoshoots in India, revolutionizing how businesses showcase themselves online.

2012 - Today

We have onboarded 10,000+ SMBs across categories on Google Maps, generating an incredible 2 billion+ views for our clients and establishing ourselves as Google's preferred vendor for large-scale projects.
