IMMERSIVE INNOVATION: TELLME'S VR EXPERIENCE CENTRE TRANSFORMS MADHYA PRADESH

Virtual Reality is redefining storytelling and cultural immersion! TellMe proudly unveils its state-of-the-art VR Experience Centres in Bhopal and Orchha, bringing history, heritage, and tourism to life like never before.

A landmark moment for immersive technology! On International Museum Day (May 18, 2025), Hon'ble Chief Minister Dr. Mohan Yadav inaugurated the 'Aankhon Dekha' Immersive Experience Centre at the MP State Museum, Bhopal-ushering in a new era of VR-powered cultural storytelling.
