Minto Hall: A New Chapter in Bhopal's Legacy

Bhopal's Minto Hall, with its famous restaurant Crown 1909 overlooking the Upper Lake, is a historical edifice once known as the Old Vidhan Sabha of Madhya Pradesh. It is now set to be transformed into an iconic MICE convention center, providing a venue for conventions, conferences, and social events like weddings. The government's initiative to develop Minto Hall into a cultural hub aligns with its architectural grandeur and its potential to attract both domestic and international visitors. Renamed as Kushabhau Thakre International Convention Centre, it now hosts major meetings, conferences, and film shoots. This majestic venue stands as a testament to Bhopal’s colonial past and a beacon for future cultural and corporate events.

Orchha: Preserving History While Embracing the Future

In the heart of India, Orchha captivates visitors with its medieval architecture near the serene Betwa River. The town is recognized for its renowned palaces and temples, such as the Jahangir Mahal and Ram Raja Temple, which continue to draw history enthusiasts and spiritual seekers alike. Recent efforts to promote Orchha as a must-visit destination ensure that its historical essence is preserved while enhancing tourist facilities.
